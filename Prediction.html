Introduction

Welcome to our webpage displaying predicted results of the upcoming CR election using machine learning. Our model has been trained on historical data and uses a variety of features to predict the outcome of the election. We will be updating the page as new data becomes available, so be sure to check back frequently!





How it Works

Our machine learning model takes into account a variety of features, including Age, GPA, Extracurricular Activities, Leadership Experience, Election Result to make its predictions. The model is constantly being trained on new data to improve its accuracy, and we believe that it provides a more accurate picture of the upcoming election than traditional polling methods.

About Us

We are a team of Students who are passionate about using machine learning to better understand and predict election outcomes. Our mission is to provide accurate and timely information to help voters make informed decisions in the election process.
